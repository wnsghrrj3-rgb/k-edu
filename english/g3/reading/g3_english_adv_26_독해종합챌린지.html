📖 지문 A — The School Garden
Last spring, Ms. Lee's class started a school garden. First, they chose a sunny spot behind the school. Then, they dug the soil and planted seeds. Next, the students watered the plants every day. Finally, after two months, beautiful flowers and fresh vegetables grew. The garden was popular because everyone could pick tomatoes and strawberries. However, taking care of the garden was not easy. Unlike indoor plants, outdoor plants needed more water and sunlight. Both teachers and students worked hard together, so the garden became the best part of the school.

📖 지문 B — The Lost Kitten
It was a dark and rainy evening. Emma heard a tiny cry outside her window. She opened the door and found a small, trembling kitten on the doorstep. Its fur was wet and it looked miserable. Emma carefully picked up the kitten and wrapped it in a warm towel. She gave it some milk. Soon, the kitten stopped shaking and started purring softly. Emma smiled and said, "I'll call you Lucky."

📖 지문 C — Save the Sea Turtles!
Sea turtles are in danger. Every year, thousands of sea turtles die because of plastic in the ocean. They eat plastic bags, thinking they are jellyfish. This makes them very sick. We must help save sea turtles! You should stop using plastic bags. Use cloth bags instead. Also, if you visit the beach, always pick up your trash. Together, we can make the ocean safe for sea turtles.
