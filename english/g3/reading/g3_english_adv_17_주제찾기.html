📖 17차시 — 주제찾기 (Finding the Main Idea)
Main Idea 🎯
글이 가장 말하고 싶은 한 문장이에요!
Main Idea vs Supporting Details
🎯 Main Idea = 글의 중심 생각 (한 문장)
📎 Supporting Details = 중심 생각을 도와주는 구체적 정보
💡 팁: Main Idea는 보통 첫 문장 또는 마지막 문장에 있어요!
💡 예시로 이해하기
Dogs are great pets. They are friendly and loyal. They can learn many tricks. They also keep you safe at home.
🎯 Main Idea:
"Dogs are great pets."
📎 Supporting Details:
① friendly and loyal
② can learn tricks
③ keep you safe

Cats are very clean animals. They wash themselves every day. They also keep their sleeping area tidy. Cats do not like dirty places.

📎 Supporting Details

Q7 ⭐⭐⭐
Bees make honey. They also help flowers grow. Without bees, many plants would die. Bees are very important for nature.
Main Idea의 빈칸을 채우세요!
Bees are very ___ for nature.
