🔮 22차시 — 추론하기 (Making Inferences)
Clues + What I Know = Inference 🧩
글에 직접 쓰여있지 않은 정보를 단서로 알아내요!
추론 = 탐정처럼 단서(clues)를 모아서 생각하기! 🕵️
💡 "글에 안 써있지만, 이런 뜻이겠구나!" 라고 짐작하는 거예요.
💡 예시로 이해하기
Mia put on her swimsuit. She packed sunscreen and a towel. She asked her mom, "Can we go now?"
🔍 단서: swimsuit, sunscreen, towel
Mia는 어디에 가려는 걸까? → 해변이나 수영장! 🏖️
글에 "beach"나 "pool"이 없지만, 단서로 추론할 수 있어요!
Q1 ⭐
Jake blew out the candles on his cake. Everyone sang "Happy Birthday."
What can you infer? 🕵️
Q2 ⭐
The dog was wagging its tail and jumping around when it saw its owner.
How does the dog feel?
Q3 ⭐⭐
Emily grabbed her umbrella and put on her rain boots before leaving the house.
단서(clue)를 모두 고르세요!
Q4 ⭐⭐
The students cheered loudly. The scoreboard showed 5-4. Their team had scored the winning goal.
What can you infer?
Q5 ⭐⭐
Mom looked at the messy room and crossed her arms. She said, "Well, well, well..."
How does Mom probably feel?
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐
Mr. Park checked his watch three times. He walked back and forth near the entrance. He kept looking at the road.
What can you infer about Mr. Park?
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐
"Please be quiet," the woman said. "Other people are trying to read." She pointed at the "No talking" sign on the wall.
Where are they? 🏢
Q8 ⭐⭐⭐
She wore a white coat and checked the patient's temperature. Then she wrote something on her clipboard and said, "Take this medicine three times a day."
What is her job?
Q9 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
The leaves on the trees were turning red and yellow. Squirrels were busy collecting nuts. The air felt cool and crisp. Children were picking pumpkins at the farm.
What season is it? And how do you know?
Q10 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Sam looked at the dark clouds and frowned. He had planned a picnic with his friends at the park. He picked up his phone and started typing a message.
추론할 수 있는 것을 모두 고르세요!
