불규칙 과거형 10개+ 마스터!
📖 과거형 만들기
규칙: 동사 + ed
play → played / watch → watched / cook → cooked
불규칙: 모양이 변해요! ⚠️
go→went eat→ate see→saw have→had make→made read→read take→took come→came get→got buy→bought
📖 과거 질문과 대답
What did you do yesterday?
→ I went to the park and played soccer.
Did you eat breakfast?
→ Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.
💡 Did 뒤에는 동사원형! (Did you go? ← went 아님!)
