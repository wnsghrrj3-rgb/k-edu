📖 계절 + 활동 + 이유
🌸 In spring, I like to ride a bike
because the weather is nice.
☀️ In summer, I like to swim
because it's hot.
🍂 In fall, I like to read books
because the leaves are beautiful.
❄️ In winter, I like to make a snowman
because it snows a lot.
