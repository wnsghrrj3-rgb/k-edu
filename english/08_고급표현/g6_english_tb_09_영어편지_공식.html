✉️ 공식 편지(Formal Letter) 구조 ⭐⭐⭐
공식 편지는 5개 파트로 구성돼요:

📌 1. Greeting — Dear Sir/Madam, / Dear Mr./Ms. ~,
📌 2. Opening — I am writing to ~ (목적)
📌 3. Body — 구체적 내용·이유·요청
📌 4. Closing — I look forward to hearing from you.
📌 5. Sign-off — Yours sincerely, / Yours faithfully,
💡 이름을 알면 Yours sincerely, 모르면 Yours faithfully!
📝 공식 편지 예시
Dear Mr. Johnson,

I am writing to ask about the summer volunteer program at your organization. I am a sixth grader at Hanbit Elementary School and I am very interested in environmental protection.

Could you please send me more information about how to apply?

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours sincerely,
Minjun Lee

📖 편지 읽기
Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to express my concern about the lack of recycling bins in our neighborhood park. I visit the park every weekend, and I often see trash on the ground because there are no proper bins for recycling.

I believe that installing recycling bins would encourage visitors to sort their waste and keep the park clean. I would be grateful if you could consider my suggestion.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours faithfully,
Jihye Kim
