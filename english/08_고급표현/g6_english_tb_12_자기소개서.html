📄 영어 자기소개서 구조 ⭐⭐⭐
특목고 대비 영어 자기소개서(Personal Statement)는 4단계로 구성:

🔹 1. Introduction — 이름 + 지원 동기
🔹 2. Strengths & Experiences — 강점 + 경험
🔹 3. Goals & Plans — 목표 + 학업 계획
🔹 4. Conclusion — 마무리 + 포부
💡 자기소개서는 구체적인 경험과 숫자가 설득력을 높여요!
✍️ 핵심 표현
📌 I am applying to ~ because ~ — ~에 지원합니다, 왜냐하면
📌 My greatest strength is ~ — 저의 가장 큰 강점은
📌 Through this experience, I learned that ~ — 이 경험을 통해 ~을 배웠습니다
📌 I am confident that I can contribute to ~ — ~에 기여할 수 있다고 확신합니다
"I am applying to Sejong International Middle School because I am passionate about global communication. Through my experience as a school English club leader, I learned that language connects people across cultures."

My name is Yuna Choi and I am applying to Daewon Foreign Language Middle School because I dream of becoming an international journalist. My greatest strength is my curiosity about different cultures. Last year, I participated in the Global Youth Forum and presented a speech about cultural diversity in English. Through this experience, I learned that understanding different perspectives is essential for effective communication. In the future, I plan to study world history and improve my writing skills. I am confident that I can contribute to the school community with my passion and dedication. Thank you for considering my application.
