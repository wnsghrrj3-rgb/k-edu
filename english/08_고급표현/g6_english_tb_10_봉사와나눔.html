📖 봉사 경험 에세이 읽기
Last winter, I volunteered at a shelter for homeless people in my city. Every Saturday morning, I helped serve breakfast and organize donated clothing. At first, I felt nervous because I did not know what to expect. However, the people at the shelter were very kind and grateful. This experience taught me that even small acts of kindness can make a big difference in someone's life. I realized that true happiness comes from helping others, not from receiving things. I plan to continue volunteering because I want to contribute to building a more caring community.
