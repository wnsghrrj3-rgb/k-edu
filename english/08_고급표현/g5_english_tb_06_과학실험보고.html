🔬 개념 학습 — 실험 보고 표현
실험 순서 표현
First → Then → Next → Finally / As a result
🔊 First, we put salt into the water.
🔊 Then, we heated the water for 10 minutes.
🔊 As a result, the salt dissolved completely.
🔬 수동태 in 과학 보고
was/were + 과거분사(p.p.)
과학 보고서에서는 수동태가 자연스러워요
🔊 The water was heated to 100 degrees.
🔊 The experiment was observed carefully.
💡 과학 핵심 동사: observe(관찰하다), measure(측정하다), dissolve(녹다), heat(가열하다)

Q8 📖 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
🔬 Experiment: Does sugar dissolve faster in hot water?

First, we prepared two cups — one with cold water and one with hot water. Then, we added one spoon of sugar to each cup. Next, we stirred both cups at the same speed. The time was measured with a stopwatch. As a result, the sugar in the hot water dissolved in 15 seconds, while the sugar in the cold water took 45 seconds.
