✨ 개념 학습 — 가정법 과거
If I were ~, I would ~
현재 사실과 다른 상상을 할 때 (2차 가정법)
🔊 If I were a bird, I would fly around the world.
🔊 If I were rich, I would build a library.
💡 주어가 I/he/she여도 were 사용! (was ❌ → were ⭕)
✨ I wish I could ~
I wish I could + 동사원형
"~할 수 있으면 좋겠다" (현재 불가능한 소망)
🔊 I wish I could speak five languages.
🔊 I wish I could travel to space.
✅ 정리: If I were ~ = 상상 / I wish ~ = 소망 / I would ~ = 결과
