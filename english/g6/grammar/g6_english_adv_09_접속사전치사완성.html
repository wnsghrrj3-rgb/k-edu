🔗 접속사·전치사 완성

despite / in spite of / due to / owing to 등 고급 표현 완전 정복!

📌 핵심 구분
① 접속사(+절): although, though, while, whereas
② 전치사(+명사/동명사): despite, in spite of, due to
③ 등위접속사: and, but, or, so, for, yet, nor
④ 상관접속사: both A and B, either A or B, neither A nor B
Q1 ⭐⭐⭐

"______ the heavy rain, we went hiking."

💡 뒤에 명사구(the heavy rain) → 전치사 Despite
절이 오면 → 접속사 Although
Q2 ⭐⭐⭐

"The game was canceled ______ it rained heavily."

Q3 ⭐⭐⭐

각 표현이 접속사인지 전치사인지 골라주세요.

A. in spite of
B. whereas
C. due to
Q4 ⭐⭐⭐

"______ Tom ______ Jane came to the party." (둘 다 안 옴)

Q5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

"______ some people prefer tea, others prefer coffee." (반면에)

Q6 ⭐⭐⭐

어법 판단: ⭕ 또는 ❌

"Although of the cold weather, she wore a dress."
💡 although + 절 / despite(in spite of) + 명사
→ "Despite the cold weather" 또는 "Although the weather was cold"
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐

"______ you study hard, you will fail the test."
(공부하지 않으면)

💡 unless = if ~ not (만약 ~하지 않으면)
Q8 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Not only the students but also the teacher ______ surprised."

💡 not only A but also B → 동사는 B에 수일치 (the teacher → was)
Q9 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

"despite"를 "although"로 바꿔 빈칸을 완성하세요.

원문: "Despite being tired, he kept working."
변환: "______ he was tired, he kept working."
