Q1 ⭐⭐⭐

"She ______ the report by tomorrow." — 미래완료 능동태로 올바른 것은?

Q2 ⭐⭐⭐

능동: "They are building a new bridge."
→ 수동: "A new bridge ______."

Q3 ⭐⭐⭐

각 문장의 시제를 골라 매칭하세요.

A. "I had been waiting for hours."
B. "She has been studying since 3 PM."
C. "They will be traveling next week."
Q4 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

단어를 올바른 순서로 배열하여 과거완료 수동태 문장을 완성하세요.

Q5 ⭐⭐⭐

다음 문장의 어법이 올바르면 ⭕, 틀리면 ❌를 고르세요.

"The homework has been completed by the students yesterday."
💡 현재완료(has been completed)는 yesterday 같은 과거 특정 시점과 함께 쓸 수 없습니다. → "was completed" (과거 수동태)
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

"By the time the police arrived, the thief ______ already ______."

Q7 ⭐⭐⭐

각 문장이 능동태인지 수동태인지 판별하세요.

A. "The cake was eaten by the children."
B. "Scientists have discovered a new species."
C. "The letter is being written right now."
Q8 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

능동태를 수동태로 전환하세요.

"Someone will deliver the package tomorrow."

→ 수동: "The package ______ tomorrow."
