Q1 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 빈출 #1 수일치

"The number of students ______ increasing every year."

💡 the number of ~ = ~의 수 (단수)
a number of ~ = 많은 (복수)
Q2 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 빈출 #2 준동사

"The teacher expected the students ______ the homework."

💡 expect + 목적어 + to-V (5형식)
Q3 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 빈출 #3 태

"The results of the experiment ______ later today."

Q4 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 빈출 #4 관계사

"This is ______ I have been looking for."

💡 선행사 없음 → what (= the thing which)
Q5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 빈출 #5 병렬

"He enjoys reading books, watching movies, and ______."

Q6 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 빈출 #6 시제

"She ______ in Seoul since 2010."

💡 since + 과거 시점 → 현재완료
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 빈출 #8 접속사

"______ the fact that he was ill, he attended the meeting."

Q8 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 빈출 #9 형/부

"The test was ______ difficult than expected."

💡 비교급 강조: much / far / even / considerably ✓
⚠️ very + 비교급 ✗
Q9 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 빈출 #10 대명사

"The climate of Korea is milder than ______ of Russia."

💡 같은 종류 불가산 명사 반복 회피 → that
가산 단수 → one / 가산 복수 → those
