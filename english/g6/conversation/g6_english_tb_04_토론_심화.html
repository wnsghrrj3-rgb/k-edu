🎤 3단 논증 구조 ⭐⭐⭐
토론에서 설득력 있게 말하려면 3단 논증을 사용해요:

🔹 1단: 주장 (Claim) — I believe that ~
🔹 2단: 근거 (Reason) — This is because ~
🔹 3단: 예시/증거 (Evidence) — For example, ~
"I believe that students should wear school uniforms. This is because uniforms reduce bullying based on clothing. For example, a study showed that schools with uniforms had 30% fewer bullying cases."
🔗 토론 전환어 체계 (Transition Words)
주장 추가: Furthermore, / Moreover, / In addition,
반론: However, / On the other hand, / Nevertheless,
예시: For instance, / For example, / Such as ~
결론: In conclusion, / Therefore, / To sum up,
양보: Although ~, / Even though ~, / Despite ~,
💡 전환어를 쓰면 논리적 흐름이 훨씬 좋아져요!

📖 토론문 읽기
I strongly believe that students should learn coding in elementary school. First of all, coding develops logical thinking skills. Furthermore, it prepares students for future careers in technology. For instance, many jobs in 2030 will require basic programming knowledge. Some people argue that elementary students are too young to learn coding. However, studies have shown that children can understand coding concepts as early as age 7. In conclusion, teaching coding in elementary school is essential for our future.
