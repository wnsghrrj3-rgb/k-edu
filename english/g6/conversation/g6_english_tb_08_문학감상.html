📖 문학 분석 기초 용어 ⭐⭐⭐
문학 작품을 분석할 때 쓰는 핵심 용어들:

📌 theme — 주제 (작품이 전달하는 큰 메시지)
📌 symbol — 상징 (다른 의미를 나타내는 사물/이미지)
📌 metaphor — 은유 (A를 B에 직접 비유)
📌 moral — 교훈 (이야기가 가르치는 교훈)
"In many stories, a dove is a symbol of peace. The theme of the story is that kindness is more powerful than anger."
🎭 은유 vs 직유
Metaphor (은유) — like/as 없이 직접 비유
→ "Time is money." (시간은 돈이다)

Simile (직유) — like/as를 사용한 비유
→ "Her smile was like sunshine." (그녀의 미소는 햇빛과 같았다)

💡 둘 다 figurative language(비유적 표현)이에요!

📖 단편 이야기 읽기
Once, there was a young tree standing alone on a hill. Every winter, the cold wind tried to break its branches. But the tree bent with the wind instead of fighting against it. Years passed, and the tree grew tall and strong. The old oak trees nearby, which had stood stiff against the storms, had all fallen. The young tree, now the tallest on the hill, whispered to the wind: "Thank you for teaching me."
