💼 직업 분석 표현 ⭐⭐⭐
직업을 분석하고 소개할 때 쓰는 표현들:

📌 ~ requires + 명사/동명사 — ~을 필요로 한다
📌 ~ involves + 동명사 — ~을 수반한다
📌 What qualities do you need? — 어떤 자질이 필요한가요?
📌 This job is suitable for people who ~ — 이 직업은 ~한 사람에게 적합하다
"Being a doctor requires years of study and dedication. It involves helping patients recover from illness."
📝 진로 에세이 구조
진로 에세이는 보통 이렇게 구성해요:

🔹 1. 꿈꾸는 직업 — My dream job is ~
🔹 2. 이유 — I am interested in ~ because ~
🔹 3. 필요한 자질/능력 — This job requires ~
🔹 4. 준비 계획 — To achieve this goal, I plan to ~
Q1 ⭐⭐⭐
직업과 필요한 자질을 매칭하세요.
👨‍⚕️ Doctor
requires patience and medical knowledge
👩‍💻 Software Engineer
involves solving complex problems with code
👩‍🏫 Teacher
requires communication skills and creativity
Q2 ⭐⭐⭐
빈칸에 알맞은 표현을 고르세요.
Being a pilot excellent eyesight and the ability to stay calm under pressure.
Q3 ⭐⭐⭐
다음 문장이 문법적으로 올바른가요?
"This job involves to help people with their legal problems."
Q4 ⭐⭐⭐
"involves"와 함께 쓸 수 있는 표현을 모두 고르세요.
💡 involves + 동명사(~ing)
working with animals
to study hard
traveling to different countries
to teach children
designing buildings
Q5 ⭐⭐⭐
"This job is suitable for people who enjoy solving puzzles." — 어떤 직업에 대한 설명일까요?
📖 진로 에세이 읽기
My dream job is to become a marine biologist. I have been fascinated by the ocean since I was young. This job requires strong science skills and the ability to swim well. It involves studying sea creatures and protecting ocean ecosystems. To achieve this goal, I plan to study biology in university and volunteer at an aquarium. I believe that understanding the ocean is essential for protecting our planet.
