📖 지문 읽기
What will the world look like in 2050? Experts predict that artificial intelligence will play a major role in our daily lives. AI is expected to help doctors diagnose diseases more accurately and assist teachers in creating personalized learning experiences. Furthermore, renewable energy sources such as solar and wind power are expected to replace most fossil fuels. If we invest in green technology now, we will have a cleaner and healthier planet. However, some people worry that AI might replace human jobs. If that happened, governments would need to create new types of employment. The future holds both great promise and serious challenges.
