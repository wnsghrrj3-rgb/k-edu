📖 지문 읽기
Changdeokgung Palace is one of the five grand palaces built during the Joseon Dynasty. It was constructed in 1405 and is located in northern Seoul. The palace is known for its beautiful garden, called Huwon. It has been a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1997. Changdeokgung is considered a great example of Korean palace architecture because it was designed to blend naturally with the surrounding landscape. Today, it attracts over 1 million visitors every year.
