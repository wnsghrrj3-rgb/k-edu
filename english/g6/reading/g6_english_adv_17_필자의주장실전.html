17차시 — 필자의 주장 실전
수능 20번형 — 필자가 주장하는 바를 파악하는 유형
핵심 전략: ① 주장 신호 표현 찾기 (should, must, need to, it is important that…)
② 마지막 1~2문장에 주장이 집중되는 경우가 많음
③ 예시·근거 vs 주장 구별하기
⭐⭐⭐ ~ ⭐⭐⭐⭐
🧠 주장 신호 표현 정리
강한 주장
should / must / have to
It is essential that…
We need to…
부드러운 주장
It is important that…
I believe / I suggest
We ought to…
결론 신호
Therefore / Thus
In conclusion
For this reason
⚠️ 함정: 예시 ≠ 주장
For example / For instance
Studies show that…
→ 근거일 뿐, 주장이 아님!
📖 지문 읽기 — Passage A
Many students spend hours studying by simply rereading their textbooks. However, research has consistently shown that this method is one of the least effective strategies for long-term retention. Active recall — the practice of testing yourself on material rather than passively reviewing it — has been proven to strengthen memory far more effectively. Students should therefore replace passive rereading with self-testing and practice questions to improve their academic performance.

📖 지문 읽기 — Passage B
In today's digital world, children are exposed to screens from a very young age. While technology can be a valuable educational tool, excessive screen time has been linked to sleep problems, reduced physical activity, and difficulties in social development. Parents must set clear boundaries on daily screen use and encourage their children to engage in outdoor play, reading, and face-to-face conversations. Only by establishing healthy habits early can we protect the well-being of the next generation.

📖 지문 읽기 — Passage C (고난도)
The growing popularity of fast fashion has created a culture of disposable clothing. Consumers purchase items they wear only a few times before discarding them, contributing to massive textile waste. While recycling programs exist, they address only a fraction of the problem. What is truly needed is a fundamental shift in consumer behavior. People should invest in fewer, higher-quality garments and view clothing as something to be valued and maintained rather than thrown away.
