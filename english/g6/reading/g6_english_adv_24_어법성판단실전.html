수능 29번형 — 밑줄 친 (A), (B), (C)에서 어법에 맞는 표현을 고르는 유형

핵심 출제 포인트 5가지:
① 수일치 · ② 능동/수동 · ③ 관계사 · ④ to부정사/동명사 · ⑤ 접속사/전치사
⭐⭐⭐ ~ ⭐⭐⭐⭐
🧠 어법 빈출 5대 포인트
① 수일치
The number of students is…
A number of students are…
② 능동/수동
주어가 행위자 → 능동
주어가 대상 → 수동(be + p.p.)
③ 관계사
who/which/that + 불완전한 문장
where/when + 완전한 문장
④ to부정사 vs 동명사
decide/want → to V
enjoy/avoid → V-ing
⑤ 접속사 vs 전치사
접속사(because, although) + S+V / 전치사(due to, despite) + 명사
Q1 ⭐⭐⭐
어법상 올바른 것은?
The number of people who visit the museum (A) is / (B) are increasing every year.
Q2 ⭐⭐⭐
어법상 올바른 것은?
The letter (A) wrote / (B) was written by a student who wanted to thank the teacher.
Q3 ⭐⭐⭐
어법상 올바른 것은?
This is the park (A) where / (B) which we used to play as children.
Q4 ⭐⭐⭐
어법상 올바른 것은?
She decided (A) studying / (B) to study abroad after graduation.
Q5 ⭐⭐⭐
어법상 올바른 것은?
(A) Although / (B) Despite she was tired, she continued working on the project.

This discovery has led researchers (C) to reconsider / reconsidering the boundaries between human and animal communication.
