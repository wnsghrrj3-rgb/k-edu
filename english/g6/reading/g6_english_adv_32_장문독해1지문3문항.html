📖 장문 (Part 1/3)
주어진 글: Emma had always dreamed of becoming a professional pianist, but her family could not afford private lessons.
📖 장문 (Part 2/3)
(A) One afternoon, she discovered an old piano in the school's storage room. The music teacher, Mr. Park, noticed her fascination and offered to teach her during lunch breaks for free. Under his guidance, Emma quickly progressed from simple scales to complex compositions.

(B) However, just weeks before the regional competition, Mr. Park fell seriously ill and could no longer coach her. Emma was devastated, but she remembered his words: "The music is already inside you. Trust your hands." She decided to prepare on her own, practicing late into the night.
📖 장문 (Part 3/3)
(C) On the day of the competition, Emma's hands trembled as she sat before the grand piano. But the moment she touched the first key, everything else disappeared. She played with a depth of emotion that moved the judges to tears. When the results were announced, Emma had won first place. She dedicated her victory to Mr. Park, the teacher who had believed in her when no one else did.
