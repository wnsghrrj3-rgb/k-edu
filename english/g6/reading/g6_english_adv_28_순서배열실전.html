수능 36~37번형 — 주어진 글 다음에 이어질 순서를 배열하는 유형

핵심 전략: ① 주어진 글의 핵심 소재 파악 → ② 지시어·연결어 단서 찾기 (this, such, however, therefore) → ③ 대명사가 가리키는 대상 추적
⭐⭐⭐ ~ ⭐⭐⭐⭐
🧠 순서 배열 핵심 단서
지시어
this/these/such/that
→ 앞에 언급된 내용 필요!
연결어
However/But → 반전
Therefore/Thus → 결론
For example → 예시
대명사
he/she/it/they
→ 앞에 사람/대상 필요!
관사
a/an → 최초 등장
the → 이미 언급된 것

주어진 글: A group of researchers wanted to test whether background music affects concentration.
(A) They divided 100 students into two groups: one studied in silence, the other with classical music playing.
(B) The results were surprising — the music group actually performed slightly better on the test.
(C) After studying for one hour, both groups took the same exam to measure their recall ability.

(C) Eventually, the work of Galileo and Kepler confirmed that Copernicus had been right all along.

(B) Yet research in psychology suggests otherwise: creativity is largely a skill that can be developed through consistent effort and the right environment.
(C) This evidence challenges the myth of the "born genius" and suggests that anyone can become more creative with practice.
