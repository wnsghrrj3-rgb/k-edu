🧠 내용 일치 문제 — 흔한 함정 3가지
① 숫자 바꿔치기
글: "founded in 1952"
선택지: "in 1925"
→ 숫자 순서 뒤바뀜!
② 부정어 삽입
글: "she attended"
선택지: "she did not attend"
→ not 추가!
③ 대상 바꾸기
글: "his mother helped"
선택지: "his father helped"
→ 주체 변경!
📖 지문 A — 인물 소개 (26번형)
Maria Chen (1985–) is a Taiwanese-American marine biologist known for her research on coral reef restoration. She earned her doctorate from Stanford University in 2012 and spent three years studying coral ecosystems in Australia's Great Barrier Reef. In 2018, she founded the nonprofit organization "Reef Hope," which has restored over 50,000 square meters of damaged coral. Chen currently serves as a professor at the University of Hawaii and lives in Honolulu with her two children.

📚 Lakewood Public Library — Summer Reading Program

• Period: June 15 – August 31, 2026
• Eligibility: All Lakewood residents with a library card
• How it works: Read at least 5 books and log them on the library app. Participants who complete the challenge receive a certificate and a free tote bag.
