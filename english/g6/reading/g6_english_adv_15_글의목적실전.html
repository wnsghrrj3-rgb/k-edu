📖 지문 읽기 — Passage A
Dear Manager,

I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with the service I received at your restaurant last Friday evening. Despite having made a reservation two weeks in advance, my family and I were kept waiting for over forty minutes before being seated. Furthermore, when our meals finally arrived, two of the dishes were incorrect. Although the waiter apologized, no effort was made to correct the situation promptly. I would appreciate it if you could address these issues and ensure that future customers do not experience similar problems.

📖 지문 읽기 — Passage B
Dear Dr. Kim,

I wanted to take a moment to thank you for your incredible support during my recovery. When I first came to your clinic, I was barely able to walk without assistance. Over the past six months, your expertise and encouragement have helped me regain not only my physical strength but also my confidence. I truly believe that without your dedication, my progress would not have been possible. I hope this letter conveys even a fraction of the gratitude I feel.
