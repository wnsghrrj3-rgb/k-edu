핵심 전략: ① 주어진 문장의 지시어·연결어 분석 → ② 삽입 후 앞뒤가 자연스러운지 확인 → ③ "이 문장이 받는 내용이 앞에 있는가?"

🧠 문장 삽입 = 순서 배열의 변형!
앞 문장 확인

💡 삽입 문장에 However/But → 앞에 반대 내용 필요!
For example → 앞에 일반론 필요!
📖 문제 A
📌 주어진 문장: "However, this convenience comes at a significant environmental cost."
① Online shopping has transformed the way people buy goods, making it possible to order almost anything from the comfort of home. ② Millions of packages are delivered daily, often wrapped in layers of plastic and cardboard. ③ The carbon emissions from delivery trucks add further strain to an already fragile environment. ④ As consumers, we must consider whether the speed of delivery is worth the damage it causes.

📖 문제 B
📌 주어진 문장: "For example, a student who fails an exam may feel embarrassed at first but ultimately develop stronger study habits."
① Psychologists have found that experiencing failure can actually lead to personal growth. ② When people face setbacks, they are forced to reflect on their actions and make adjustments. ③ Over time, these adjustments build resilience — the ability to recover from difficulties. ④ This is why many successful individuals credit their failures as turning points in their lives.

"For example"로 시작하므로, 바로 앞에 일반적인 진술이 있어야 한다.
📖 문제 C (고난도)
📌 주어진 문장: "Yet the very features that make it so efficient also make it vulnerable to manipulation."
① The internet has become the primary source of information for billions of people worldwide. ② Search engines can deliver answers to almost any question in a fraction of a second, and social media allows news to spread at an unprecedented speed. ③ False information can be designed to look just as credible as real news, making it difficult for users to tell the difference. ④ This growing problem has led governments and tech companies to invest in tools for detecting and combating misinformation.
