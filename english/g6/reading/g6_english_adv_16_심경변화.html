16차시 — 심경 변화
수능 19번형 — 등장인물의 감정·분위기 변화를 파악하는 유형
핵심 전략: ① 전반부 감정 어휘 포착 → ② 전환 신호(but, however, then) 찾기 → ③ 후반부 감정 변화 확인

😰 anxious → 😌 relieved
⭐⭐⭐ ~ ⭐⭐⭐⭐
🧠 감정 어휘 미리보기
부정 감정
anxious · frustrated · terrified
desperate · embarrassed · gloomy
긍정 감정
relieved · delighted · grateful
confident · thrilled · peaceful
중립/혼합
curious · surprised · confused
indifferent · skeptical · puzzled
전환 신호어
but · however · then
suddenly · to my surprise · finally
📖 지문 읽기 — Passage A
Sarah stood backstage, her hands trembling and her heart pounding so loudly she was sure everyone could hear it. She had never performed in front of such a large audience before. As her name was called, she walked onto the stage with shaky legs. But the moment she began to sing, something changed. The melody flowed naturally, and the audience listened in complete silence. When the final note faded, the hall erupted in applause. A wide smile spread across her face, and for the first time that evening, she felt truly alive.
Q1 ⭐⭐⭐
Sarah의 심경 변화로 가장 적절한 것은?
Q2 ⭐⭐⭐
전반부에서 Sarah의 긴장감을 보여주는 표현은?
Q3 ⭐⭐⭐
감정 전환을 알리는 신호어: "___ the moment she began to sing, something changed."
📖 지문 읽기 — Passage B
Tom arrived at the abandoned house at midnight, his flashlight barely cutting through the thick darkness. Every creak of the old wooden floor made him freeze in terror. He was convinced that something was watching him from the shadows. Then, as he pushed open a door at the end of the hallway, he found a small kitten curled up on a dusty blanket, mewing softly. He let out a long breath he didn't know he had been holding and gently picked up the tiny creature.
Q4 ⭐⭐⭐
Tom의 심경 변화로 가장 적절한 것은?
Q5 ⭐⭐
"He let out a long breath"는 Tom이 더 긴장했다는 의미이다.
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
다음 감정 어휘 중 Passage B 후반부의 분위기와 가장 가까운 것은?

Q8 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
다음 글 전체를 읽고 심경 변화를 고르세요.
Maya had been dreading the math test all week. As the papers were handed out, her mind went completely blank. She couldn't remember a single formula. However, after taking a deep breath, she noticed that the first question was one she had practiced dozens of times. Slowly, her memory returned, and she began writing with growing confidence. By the time she reached the last question, she felt a quiet sense of pride.
