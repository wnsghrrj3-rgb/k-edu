규칙과 의무
must (꼭 해야 해) vs should (하는 게 좋아)
+ Don't ~ (하지 마!)
📖 must / should / Don't
🚨 You must wear a seatbelt.
꼭 해야 해! (규칙·법·안전)
💡 You should drink more water.
하는 게 좋아! (조언·권유)
🚫 Don't run in the hallway!
하지 마! (금지)
💡 must not = 절대 안 돼! / should not = 안 하는 게 좋아
📖 학교 규칙 예시
🚨 You must listen to the teacher.
🚨 You must not use your phone.
💡 You should be kind to friends.
🚫 Don't eat in the classroom!
