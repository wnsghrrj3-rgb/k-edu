📖 개념 2 — 의문문 어순
의문사 + 조동사/be + 주어 + 동사?
❓ What do you like?
📍 Where does she live?
📅 When did you arrive?
💡 How + 형용사 = 확장!
How old / How many / How much / How often
📖 개념 3 — Who가 주어일 때
Who + 동사? (do/does 없이!)
🪟 Who broke the window? (누가 깼어?)
🍦 Who wants ice cream? (누가 원해?)
⚠️ Who가 주어면 do/does가 필요 없어요!
Who does want? ❌ → Who wants? ✅
