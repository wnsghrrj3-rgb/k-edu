📖 과거진행형 (Past Progressive)이란?
was / were + ~ing
"과거 특정 시점에 ~하고 있었다"
주어was/were예시 I / He / She / ItwasI was reading a book. You / We / TheywereThey were playing soccer.
🕐 "어제 8시에 뭐 하고 있었어?"
→ "I was watching TV at 8 o'clock last night."
→ "She was sleeping when I called."
📖 when과 while — 두 사건의 만남!
when + 과거 / while + 과거진행
긴 동작(진행) 중에 짧은 동작(과거)이 끼어들어요!
🔑 when + 짧은 동작 (과거)
"I was sleeping when the phone rang." (자고 있었는데 전화가 울렸다)

🔑 while + 긴 동작 (과거진행)
"While I was cooking, my brother came home." (요리하는 동안 동생이 왔다)

🔑 두 동작 동시 진행:
"While Mom was cooking, Dad was cleaning."
📖 과거진행형 3가지 형태
형태구조예시 긍정주어 + was/were + ~ingHe was studying. 부정주어 + was/were + not + ~ingHe was not studying. 의문Was/Were + 주어 + ~ing?Was he studying?
💡 축약: was not = wasn't / were not = weren't
"They weren't listening to the teacher."
