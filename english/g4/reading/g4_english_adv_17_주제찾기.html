독해 전략 1 — 주제 찾기

Main Idea vs Supporting Details

4학년 Track B-2 · 17차시

📖 개념 1 — Main Idea (주제)란?
Main Idea = 글 전체의 핵심 메시지
"이 글은 한마디로 뭐에 대한 글이야?"

🎯 Main Idea

글의 핵심 주제
하나의 문장으로 요약

📋 Supporting Details

주제를 뒷받침하는
예시, 설명, 근거

📖 개념 2 — 주제문 찾는 팁!
💡 주제문은 보통 첫 문장 또는 마지막 문장에 있어요!
Dogs are amazing pets. They are loyal and friendly. They can learn tricks. They also protect your home.
👆 첫 문장이 주제문! 나머지는 "왜 좋은지" 설명하는 Supporting Details
Q1 ⭐
Bananas are a healthy snack. They have vitamins. They give you energy. They taste sweet.
이 글의 Main Idea는?
Q2 ⭐
Spring is a beautiful season. Flowers bloom everywhere. Birds sing in the trees. The weather becomes warm and sunny.
이 글의 주제는?
Q3 ⭐⭐
Exercise is important for your health. It makes your heart strong. It helps you sleep better. It also makes you feel happy.
다음 중 Supporting Detail은?
Q4 ⭐⭐
Some students walk to school. Others take the bus. A few ride their bikes. Students use many different ways to get to school.
이 글의 Main Idea는 어디에?
Q5 ⭐⭐
Whales are the largest animals on Earth. Blue whales can grow up to 30 meters long. They eat tiny creatures called krill. A baby whale is already 7 meters at birth.
가장 좋은 제목은?
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐
Robots are changing our world. They build cars in factories. They clean houses. Some robots even help doctors in hospitals. In the future, robots will do even more jobs.
이 글의 Main Idea는?
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐
Reading books has many benefits. It improves your vocabulary. It helps you understand other people's feelings. It also makes you more creative. Reading before bed can even help you sleep better.
다음 중 Main Idea가 아닌 것은?

Korea has four beautiful seasons. In spring, cherry blossoms cover the streets. Summer brings hot weather and fun at the beach. Fall paints the mountains in red and gold. Winter covers everything in white snow.
