독해 전략 5 — 비교와 대조

공통점과 차이점 찾기

4학년 Track B-2 · 21차시

📖 개념 — 비교(Compare) & 대조(Contrast)

🤝 Compare

공통점
both, also, like, similarly, too

⚡ Contrast

차이점
but, however, unlike, while, on the other hand

Cats and dogs are both popular pets. However, cats are more independent, while dogs need more attention.
Q1 ⭐
Apples and oranges are both fruits. However, apples are crunchy, while oranges are juicy.
공통점(Compare)은?
Q2 ⭐
Soccer and basketball are both team sports. But soccer is played on a field, while basketball is played in a gym.
차이점(Contrast)은?
Q3 ⭐⭐
Summer and winter are very different. In summer, the days are long and hot. In winter, the days are short and cold. However, both seasons are great for family trips.
"however"가 알려주는 것은?
Q4 ⭐⭐
Trains and airplanes both carry many passengers. Trains travel on the ground, while airplanes fly in the sky. Trains are usually cheaper, but airplanes are much faster.
글에 따르면 올바른 것은?
Q5 ⭐⭐
"___" 뒤에는 차이점이 온다.
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐
City life and country life are quite different. In the city, there are many shops, restaurants, and buses. In the country, there are farms, forests, and quiet roads. However, people in both places enjoy spending time with friends and family.
이 글이 보여주는 구조는?
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐
E-books and paper books both let you enjoy stories. E-books are lighter and can store thousands of books. On the other hand, paper books don't need electricity and are easier on the eyes. Some people like the smell of paper books, while others prefer the convenience of e-books.
글의 내용과 다른 것은?
Q8 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Cats and dogs make wonderful pets, but they have very different personalities. Dogs are loyal and love to follow their owners everywhere. Cats, however, are more independent and enjoy being alone. Both animals need love and care. Dogs need daily walks, while cats are happy to stay indoors. Similarly, both can learn to understand simple commands.
비교(공통점) 2가지를 모두 포함한 것은?
