독해 전략 6 — 추론하기

글에 직접 안 나온 정보 유추하기

4학년 Track B-2 · 22차시

📖 개념 — 추론(Inference)이란?
글의 단서 + 내 배경지식 = 추론!
글에 직접 쓰여 있지 않지만, 단서를 통해 알아낼 수 있는 정보예요.
Mina put on her boots and grabbed her umbrella before leaving the house.
🧠 추론: 밖에 비가 오고 있거나 올 예정이다! (글에 "rain"이 없지만 알 수 있어요)
Q1 ⭐
Tom blew out the candles on his cake while everyone sang.
What can you infer? (추론할 수 있는 것은?)
Q2 ⭐
She yawned three times and could barely keep her eyes open.
How does she probably feel?
Q3 ⭐⭐
The man wore a white coat and checked the patient's heartbeat with a stethoscope.
The man is probably a ___.
Q4 ⭐⭐
Jiho looked at the scoreboard and jumped up and down with his teammates. They were all smiling and hugging each other.
What probably happened?
Q5 ⭐⭐
The leaves were turning red and orange. People wore jackets and drank warm coffee.
What season is it most likely?
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐
Sora stared at the math problem for a long time. She erased her answer twice and sighed deeply. She looked at the clock — only five minutes left.
How is Sora most likely feeling?
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐
The old woman sat on the park bench every afternoon. She always brought bread crumbs in a small bag. Within minutes, dozens of birds would gather around her feet.
Why did the birds gather around her?
Q8 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
When Minho came home, the house was dark and quiet. Suddenly, the lights turned on and he heard "Surprise!" His friends and family were standing in the living room with balloons and a large cake. Minho's eyes widened and he covered his mouth with both hands.
다음 중 추론할 수 있는 것을 모두 포함한 것은?
