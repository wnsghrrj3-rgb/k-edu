독해 전략 3 — 순서 배열

First · Then · Next · Finally

4학년 Track B-2 · 19차시

📖 개념 — 순서 신호어 (Sequence Words)
First 먼저 Then 그 다음 Next 그 후에 After that 그 후에 Finally 마지막으로
First, I wake up. Then, I brush my teeth. Next, I eat breakfast. Finally, I go to school.
💡 이 단어들이 보이면 "시간 순서"로 정리하세요!
Q1 ⭐
___, wash your hands. Then, set the table. Next, serve the food. Finally, enjoy your meal!
빈칸에 알맞은 것은?
Q2 ⭐
First, she put on her shoes. Then, she opened the door. Next, she walked to the bus stop. ___, the bus arrived.
빈칸에 알맞은 것은?
Q3 ⭐⭐
아침 준비 순서로 올바른 것은?
Q4 ⭐⭐
How to make a sandwich: First, get two slices of bread. Then, put cheese and ham on one slice. Next, add lettuce and tomato. Finally, put the other slice on top.
What do you do after adding ham and cheese?
Q5 ⭐⭐
Mina planted a flower. First, she dug a hole. Then, she put the seed in. Next, she covered it with soil. After that, she watered it. Finally, she waited for it to grow.
What did Mina do right BEFORE watering?
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐
올바른 순서로 배열하면?
A) Then, he brushed his teeth.
B) Finally, he went to school.
C) First, Jiho woke up at 7.
D) Next, he ate breakfast.
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐
Tom wanted to bake a cake. First, he mixed flour, sugar, and eggs. Then, he poured the batter into a pan. Next, he baked it in the oven for 30 minutes. Finally, he decorated it with frosting.
What was the LAST step?

C) Finally, they roasted marshmallows by the fire.
D) Then, they unpacked their bags from the car.
