Opinion Essay 쓰기

주장 + 이유 2개 + 결론 구조로
설득력 있는 에세이를 써봅니다.

난이도: ⭐⭐⭐ ~ ⭐⭐⭐⭐
📐 Opinion Essay 구조
① 서론
Introduction
Hook (관심 끌기) + Thesis Statement (주장)
예: I believe that school uniforms should be required.
② 이유 1
Body 1
Topic sentence + Supporting detail + Example
예: First, uniforms promote equality among students.
③ 이유 2
Body 2
Topic sentence + Supporting detail + Example
예: Second, they help students focus on learning.
④ 결론
Conclusion
Restate opinion + Final thought
예: In conclusion, uniforms benefit all students.

핵심: 주장 → 이유1 → 이유2 → 결론 4단 구조!

🔑 필수 표현 모음
🟢 주장 표현
• I believe that ~
• In my opinion, ~
• I strongly think ~
🟡 이유 연결
• First, / Firstly, ~
• Second, / Moreover, ~
• For example, ~
🔵 근거 강화
• This is because ~
• According to ~
• Studies show that ~
🔴 결론 표현
• In conclusion, ~
• For these reasons, ~
• Therefore, I believe ~
