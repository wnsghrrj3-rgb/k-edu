🔥 어법 실전연습 2

고난도 어법 문제 + 상세 해설로 B-1 문법 완성!

이번 차시는 실전 모의고사 수준의 고난도 문제입니다.
틀려도 괜찮아요 — 해설을 꼼꼼히 읽는 것이 핵심!
Q1 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 틀린 것은?

The professor, along with ①her students, ②were ③invited to the ④international conference.
💡 "along with her students"는 삽입구 → 진짜 주어 = The professor (단수)
→ was invited
Q2 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

(A), (B), (C)에 들어갈 표현은?

The novel, (A)[writing / written] in the 19th century, remains one of the (B)[most influential / more influential] works. It is (C)[what / that] every student should read.
💡 (A) 소설은 쓰여진 것 → written
(B) one of the + 최상급 → most influential
(C) 선행사 없이 "~하는 것" → what
Q3 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 틀린 것은?

She insisted that every member ①attends the meeting, ②despite the fact that some of ③them lived ④far away.
💡 insist/suggest/demand + that + 주어 + 동사원형 (당위의 should 생략)
→ that every member attend
Q4 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

빈칸에 가장 알맞은 것은?

Hardly had she entered the room ______ the phone rang.
💡 Hardly had S p.p. when S V
= No sooner had S p.p. than S V
= Scarcely had S p.p. when/before S V
Q5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 올바른 것은?

💡 ② It is essential that + 주어 + 동사원형(be) — 당위 표현
③ lay(놓다) 맞지만 현재 3인칭 → lays
④ very + 비교급 ✗
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

(A), (B), (C)에 들어갈 표현은?

If she (A)[had studied / studied] harder, she (B)[would pass / would have passed] the test. But now she has to (C)[retake / retaking] it.
💡 (A)(B) 가정법 과거완료 → had studied / would have passed
(C) have to + 동사원형 → retake
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 틀린 것은?

①Those who are ②interested in ③applied should submit ④their applications by Friday.
💡 "interested in + V-ing" → applying
applied는 과거분사로 전치사 in의 목적어가 될 수 없음
Q8 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

빈칸에 가장 알맞은 것은?

The more carefully you plan, ______ you will achieve your goals.
💡 the 비교급 ~, the 비교급 ~ 구문
→ The more carefully ~, the more easily ~
Q9 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

어법상 올바른 문장은?

💡 ② Neither of ~ → 단수 동사(was) ✓
① furniture 불가산 → is
③ 지각동사 see + 목적어 + 원형(cross)
④ I wish + 가정법 과거(could)
Q10 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 틀린 것은?

It was ①not until midnight ②that the missing child ③was found, ④sleeping peaceful under a tree.
💡 sleeping을 수식하는 것은 부사
→ peaceful(형용사) → peacefully(부사)
※ It was not until ~ that … 강조구문은 올바름
