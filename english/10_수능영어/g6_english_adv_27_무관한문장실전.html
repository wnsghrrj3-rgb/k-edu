📖 지문 A
① Sleep plays a crucial role in memory consolidation, the process by which short-term memories are transformed into long-term ones.
② During deep sleep, the brain replays information learned during the day, strengthening neural connections.
③ The average adult needs between seven and nine hours of sleep per night for optimal health.
④ Studies have shown that students who sleep well after studying perform significantly better on tests.
⑤ Therefore, getting adequate sleep is one of the most effective — and most overlooked — study strategies.
