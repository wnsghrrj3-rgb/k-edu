🧠 함축 의미 — 비유 표현 유형
은유 (Metaphor)
"Time is money."
→ 시간 = 돈처럼 소중함
관용 표현
"hit the nail on the head"
→ 정확히 맞추다
추상적 진술
"the map is not the territory"
→ 이론 ≠ 현실

📖 지문 읽기 — Passage A
A young artist spent years perfecting her technique by copying the masters. Her reproductions were flawless — every brushstroke identical to the originals. Yet when she finally held her own exhibition, critics were unimpressed. One reviewer wrote, "She has built a beautiful cage and locked herself inside it." It was only after she abandoned imitation and began experimenting with her own style that her work gained recognition.
💡 밑줄: "She has built a beautiful cage and locked herself inside it."

While this provides a sense of security, it can also prevent them from thinking independently. As one manager put it, "We hand them a compass but take away their ability to read the stars." True growth in any organization comes when individuals learn to navigate uncertainty on their own.

Scientists often warn that relying on a single measurement to evaluate a complex system is dangerous. A country's GDP, for example, may rise while its citizens' quality of life declines. As one economist noted, "We are counting the trees but losing sight of the forest." Without a broader set of indicators, policymakers risk making decisions that look good on paper but fail in practice.
