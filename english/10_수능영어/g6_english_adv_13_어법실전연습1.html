📌 문제 유형

Q1 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 틀린 것은?

The boy ①who lives next door ②have a dog ③that can do many ④amazing tricks.
💡 주어 = The boy (단수) → has
"who lives next door"는 삽입 수식절
Q2 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

(A), (B), (C)에 들어갈 표현으로 알맞은 것은?

The cake (A)[baking / baked] by my grandmother was delicious. She (B)[has been / had been] cooking since morning, and everyone (C)[enjoyed / was enjoyed] it.
💡 (A) 케이크는 구워진 것 → baked (과거분사)
(B) 과거 시점 이전부터 → had been
(C) everyone이 enjoy하는 것 → 능동 enjoyed
Q3 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 틀린 것은?

①Despite the heavy traffic, she ②arrived at the office on time, ③which ④surprising her boss.
💡 which surprised her boss — 계속적 용법의 주격 관계대명사 + 동사
surprising은 분사가 아닌 동사가 필요 → surprised
Q4 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

빈칸에 가장 알맞은 것은?

Not only ______ the exam, but he also got the highest score in the class.
💡 Not only + 도치(조동사+주어) → did he pass

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 올바른 것은?

Q6 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

(A), (B), (C)에 들어갈 표현으로 알맞은 것은?

If I (A)[were / was / am] you, I (B)[will / would / could have] accept the offer. The job seems (C)[interested / interesting / interestingly].
💡 (A) 가정법 과거 → were
(B) 주절 → would
(C) seems + 형용사, 주어=job(사물) → interesting
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 틀린 것은?

①What he said ②made everyone ③to feel ④uncomfortable.
💡 사역동사 make + 목적어 + 동사원형 (to 없이!)

밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 틀린 것은?

The research, ①which was ②conducting by a team of scientists, ③revealed that climate change ④affects every continent.
