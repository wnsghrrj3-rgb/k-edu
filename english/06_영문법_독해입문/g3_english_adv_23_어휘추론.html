🔎 23차시 — 어휘 추론 (Vocabulary in Context)
모르는 단어 → 문맥으로 추측! 🧩
주변 단어와 문장이 단서(clue)가 돼요!
전략 3가지:
1️⃣ 앞뒤 문장을 읽어보기
2️⃣ 같은 뜻 단어(동의어)가 있는지 찾기
3️⃣ 반대 뜻 단어(반의어)로 짐작하기
💡 예시로 이해하기
The dog was famished. It hadn't eaten all day, so it ran to its food bowl quickly.
🔍 단서: "hadn't eaten all day" + "ran to food bowl"
💡 famished = very hungry (매우 배고픈)
Q1 ⭐
It was very chilly outside. Everyone wore thick coats and gloves.
"chilly"는 무슨 뜻일까요?
Q2 ⭐
The baby was exhausted after playing all day. She fell asleep in two minutes.
"exhausted"는 무슨 뜻일까요?
Q3 ⭐⭐
The enormous elephant was much bigger than all the other animals at the zoo.
"enormous" means ___
Q4 ⭐⭐
Tom felt anxious before the test. His hands were shaking and his heart was beating fast.
"anxious"의 뜻을 알려주는 단서(clue)는?
Q5 ⭐⭐
Unlike her shy brother, Emma was very outgoing. She loved talking to everyone.
"outgoing"은 "shy"의 반대! 뜻은?
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐
The old house looked abandoned. The windows were broken, the yard was full of weeds, and no one had lived there for years.
"abandoned" most likely means ___
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐
"Don't devour your food," said Mom. "Eat slowly and chew carefully."
"devour"는 무슨 뜻일까요?
Q8 ⭐⭐⭐
The sunset was magnificent. It was so beautiful that everyone stopped to take pictures.
"magnificent"과 가장 비슷한 뜻은?
Q9 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
The explorer had to navigate through the thick jungle. He used a map and a compass to find his way. Without these tools, he would have been lost.
"navigate" most likely means ___

The swift rabbit ran across the field in seconds. Meanwhile, the timid mouse hid behind a rock, too scared to move. A peculiar bird with bright purple feathers sat on a strange-looking tree.
각 단어의 뜻을 매칭하세요!
swift → ?
timid → ?
peculiar → ?
