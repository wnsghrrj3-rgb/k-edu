💡 단어가 분위기를 만들어요!
The bright sun was shining. Birds were singing happily. Children were laughing in the park.
분위기 = 😊 Happy & Cheerful
💡 bright, singing, laughing → 밝고 행복한 느낌!

이 글의 분위기는?

이 글의 분위기는?
Q3 ⭐⭐

이 글의 분위기는?
Q4 ⭐⭐
The gentle breeze blew softly. The calm lake reflected the blue sky. Everything was still and quiet.
