접속사
and/but/or/so/because + when/before/after
🧩 등위접속사 (같은 레벨 연결)
and
그리고
I like apples and oranges.
but
하지만
I like cats but he likes dogs.
or
또는
Tea or coffee?
so
그래서
I was tired so I slept.
🧩 종속접속사 (이유·시간)
because
왜냐하면
I'm happy because I got 100.
when
~할 때
When I got home, I ate dinner.
before
~하기 전에
Wash hands before you eat.
after
~한 후에
After school, I play soccer.
