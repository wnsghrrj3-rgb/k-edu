play → played   like → liked
📌 자음+y → y를 i로: study → studied
📌 단모음+자음 → 자음 겹침: stop → stopped
📌 -e로 끝남 → d만: like → liked

🔄 did 부정문 & 의문문
❌ 부정: did not (didn't) + 동사원형
I didn't go to school. (went ❌)
❓ 의문: Did + 주어 + 동사원형?
Did you eat lunch? (ate ❌)
