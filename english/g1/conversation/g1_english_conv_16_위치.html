🚨 포인트 2 — 위치 문장은 4박자!

주어 + be동사 + 전치사 + the + 명사 순서!

⭕ The book is on the table. 💡 is + on + the + table = 4박자 완벽! 📚🟫
⭕ The cat is under the chair. 💡 is + under + the + chair = 완벽! 🐱🪑
❌ Book on table. 💡 be동사 is 빠짐! the도 빠짐! "The book is on the table" ⭕
❌ The book is on table. 💡 on 뒤에 the 빠짐! "on the table" ⭕
