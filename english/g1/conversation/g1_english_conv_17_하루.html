🚨 포인트 2 — in the 시간 vs at night!

아침·오후·저녁엔 in the ~, 밤만 at night!

⭕ I wake up in the morning. 💡 morning·afternoon·evening → in the + 시간!
⭕ I sleep at night. 💡 night만 특별! at night ⭕ 꼭 기억!
❌ I sleep in night. 💡 night엔 in 안 써! "at night" ⭕
❌ I wake up morning. 💡 in the 빠짐! "in the morning" ⭕
