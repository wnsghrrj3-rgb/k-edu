📘 분사구문이란?
부사절(접속사 + 주어 + 동사)을 ~ing로 줄인 것!
1Because she was tired, she went to bed early.
↓ 접속사 생략
2She was tired → Being tired, she went to bed early.
↓ Being 생략 가능
3Tired, she went to bed early.
💡 주어가 같으면 앞 절의 주어 + 접속사를 빼고 동사를 ~ing로!
📘 분사구문 만드는 3단계
① 접속사 삭제 → ② 주어 삭제(같을 때) → ③ 동사 → V-ing

시간 (When/While)

While I walked → Walking along, I saw a cat.

이유 (Because/As)

Because he felt sick → Feeling sick, he stayed home.

🔊 Walking along the street, I found a coin.
