📘 관계부사란?
두 문장을 연결하면서 장소·시간·이유·방법을 나타내는 접속 장치!
관계부사선행사= 전치사+관계대명사
where장소 (the place)in/at which
when시간 (the time)at/in which
why이유 (the reason)for which
how방법 (the way)in which
⚠️ the way와 how는 함께 못 씀! (the way how ❌)
📘 관계부사 예문
🔊 This is the park where I play soccer.
🔊 I remember the day when we first met.
🔊 Tell me the reason why you were late.
🔊 This is how I solved the problem.
💡 관계부사 = "접속사 + 부사" 역할을 동시에!
