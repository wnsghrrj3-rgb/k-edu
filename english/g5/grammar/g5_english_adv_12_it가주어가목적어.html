📘 it 가주어 구문
주어가 너무 길면 → it을 앞에, 진짜 주어는 뒤로!
To learn English is important. ← 주어가 길다
↓
It is important to learn English.
It is + 형용사 + to V / that절
It is easy to make mistakes.
It is true that she left.
📘 it 가목적어 구문
목적어가 너무 길면 → it을 목적어 자리에, 진짜 목적어는 뒤로!
I find to wake up early difficult. ← 목적어가 길다
↓
I find it difficult to wake up early.
S + find/make/think + it + 형용사 + to V
She made it clear that she disagreed.
