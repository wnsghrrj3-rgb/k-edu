🧭 개념 학습 — 직업과 능력
What skills do you need?
직업에 필요한 능력을 묻고 답하는 표현
🔊 What skills do you need to be a doctor?
🔊 You need to be patient and good at science.
💡 능력 표현: creative(창의적), patient(인내심), responsible(책임감), good at ~(~를 잘하는)
🧭 조건 표현
If you want to be ~, you need to ~
🔊 If you want to be a programmer, you need to learn coding.
🔊 To become a teacher, you should be patient and kind.
🔊 A firefighter requires courage and physical strength.
