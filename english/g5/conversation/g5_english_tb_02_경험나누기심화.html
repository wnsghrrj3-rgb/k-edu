현재완료진행형
have/has been + ~ing → "~해 오고 있다"
🔊 I have been studying English for 3 years.
🔊 She has been playing piano since she was five.
💡 for + 기간 (for 3 years) / since + 시점 (since 2020)
📖 최상급 경험 표현
The best ~ I've ever + p.p.
"내가 ~한 것 중 최고의"
🔊 It was the best movie I've ever watched.
🔊 This is the most delicious food I've ever tasted.
✅ ever = "지금까지" → 현재완료와 함께 사용
