📖 개념 학습
I want to be ~ because ~
자신의 꿈과 그 이유를 논리적으로 말하는 표현
🔊 I want to be a scientist because I love experiments.
🔊 My dream is to become a teacher because I enjoy helping others learn.
💡 이유를 2개 이상 연결할 때: because ~ and also ~
🔊 I want to be a vet because I love animals and also I want to help sick pets.
📖 핵심 표현 정리
꿈 표현 패턴 3가지
🔊 ① I want to be a pilot.
🔊 ② My dream is to become an artist.
🔊 ③ I would like to be a chef in the future.
✅ 이유 연결어: because / since / as
First, ~ . Second, ~ . Also, ~
