📜 개념 학습 — 과거 수동태
was/were + 과거분사 + by ~
역사 속 발명·사건을 서술하는 핵심 패턴
🔊 The telephone was invented by Alexander Graham Bell.
🔊 Hangul was created by King Sejong in 1443.
🔊 The Korean War happened in 1950.
📜 역사 서술 핵심 표현
역사 시간 표현
🔊 ① in + 연도: in 1969, in 2002
🔊 ② ~ ago: 500 years ago
🔊 ③ during + 시기: during the Joseon Dynasty
🔊 ④ ~ century: in the 15th century
💡 핵심 동사: invented(발명), discovered(발견), built(건설), established(설립)
