📬 글의 목적 파악이란?
Purpose of the Text
글쓴이가 이 글을 쓴 이유(목적)를 파악하는 유형 — 수능 18번
핵심 전략:
① 글의 형식 확인 (편지? 안내문? 이메일?)
② 첫 문장과 마지막 문장에 목적이 집중
③ 핵심 동사 찾기: ask, inform, invite, complain, thank, recommend…
to inform 알리다 to complain 불만 제기 to request 요청하다 to invite 초대하다 to thank 감사하다 to advertise 홍보하다
🔍 목적별 핵심 표현
📢 안내/공지 (to inform): "We are pleased to announce…" "Please note that…"

😤 불만 (to complain): "I am writing to express my dissatisfaction…" "I was disappointed…"

🙏 요청 (to request): "I would appreciate it if you could…" "Could you please…"

🎉 초대 (to invite): "You are cordially invited to…" "We would love for you to join…"

❤️ 감사 (to thank): "I am writing to express my gratitude…" "Thank you for…"

Dear Mr. Johnson,
I am writing to let you know that our annual school science fair will be held on May 15th. We would be honored if you could attend as a guest judge. Please let us know by April 30th.
Sincerely, Ms. Park
