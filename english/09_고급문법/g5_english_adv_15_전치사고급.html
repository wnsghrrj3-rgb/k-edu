📘 고급 전치사구 정리
전치사구의미예문
in spite of~에도 불구하고In spite of the rain, we went out.
instead of~ 대신에Instead of coffee, I had tea.
according to~에 따르면According to the news, it will snow.
due to~ 때문에The game was canceled due to rain.
in addition to~에 더하여In addition to English, she speaks French.
in front of~ 앞에He stood in front of the class.
💡 전치사 뒤에는 항상 명사/동명사!
