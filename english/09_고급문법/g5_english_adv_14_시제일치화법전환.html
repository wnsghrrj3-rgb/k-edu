📘 시제 일치 규칙
주절이 과거면 → 종속절도 한 칸 뒤로!
직접화법→간접화법
현재 (am/is)→과거 (was/were)
과거 (did)→과거완료 (had done)
will→would
can→could
💡 this→that, here→there, today→that day, now→then
📘 직접화법 → 간접화법

직접화법

He said, "I am happy."

간접화법

He said that he was happy.

의문문

"Are you tired?" → He asked if I was tired.

명령문

"Close the door." → He told me to close the door.
