📘 4형식 수동태
4형식: S + V + IO(간접목적어) + DO(직접목적어)
능동: He gave me a book.
↓
수동①: I was given a book (by him). ← IO 주어
수동②: A book was given to me (by him). ← DO 주어
💡 DO가 주어가 되면 전치사(to/for)가 필요!
📘 5형식 수동태 + by 이외 전치사
능동: They call him "Genius".
↓
수동: He is called "Genius". ← 목적보어 유지!
by 이외 전치사
be interested in / be known for
be surprised at / be covered with
be made of (재료) / be made from (원료)
