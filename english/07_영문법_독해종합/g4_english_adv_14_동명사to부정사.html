동명사와 to부정사 기초

enjoy ~ing · want to ~ · 동사별 구분

4학년 Track B-1 · 14차시

📖 개념 1 — 동명사 (~ing)
동사 + ing = 명사처럼!
"~하는 것"이라는 뜻으로 목적어에 쓰여요
📚 I enjoy reading books.
✅ She finished doing homework.
🔑 동명사만 쓰는 동사: enjoy, finish, mind, keep, practice, stop
📖 개념 2 — to부정사 (to + 동사원형)
to + 동사원형 = "~하는 것 / ~하기 위해"
🍕 I want to eat pizza.
✈️ She decided to study abroad.
🔑 to부정사만 쓰는 동사: want, need, decide, hope, plan, learn
📖 개념 3 — 어떤 동사 뒤에 뭘 쓸까?

동명사 (-ing)

enjoy, finish
mind, keep
practice, stop

to부정사 (to~)

want, need
decide, hope
plan, learn

⚠️ enjoy to swim ❌ → enjoy swimming ✅
want swimming ❌ → want to swim ✅
