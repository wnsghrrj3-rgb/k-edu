문단 쓰기 — 묘사문

사물·장소·인물 묘사 문단 완성

4학년 Track B-3 · 30차시

📖 개념 — 묘사문(Descriptive Writing)이란?
오감(Five Senses)을 사용해서 생생하게!
👁️ See 보다 👂 Hear 듣다 👃 Smell 맡다 👅 Taste 맛보다 🖐️ Touch 느끼다
My grandmother's garden is a magical place. Colorful roses and sunflowers fill every corner. Bees buzz softly around the flowers. The air smells sweet like honey. The grass feels cool and soft under my feet.
Q1 ⭐
"The chocolate cake was warm and soft."
이 문장은 어떤 감각?
Q2 ⭐
"Birds sang sweetly in the morning."
이 문장은 어떤 감각?
Q3 ⭐⭐
장소 묘사: "The beach was ___."
가장 생생한 묘사는?
Q4 ⭐⭐
인물 묘사에 어울리지 않는 문장은?
My best friend Mina has long black hair and bright eyes. She always wears a colorful headband.
Q5 ⭐⭐
"The pizza was ___" — 맛(Taste) 묘사로 가장 좋은 것은?
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐
"nice"를 더 생생한 단어로 바꾸면?
"The park was nice."
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐
My classroom is a busy place. Colorful posters cover the walls. Students' voices fill the air during group work. The smell of fresh markers comes from the whiteboard. The wooden desks feel smooth under my hands.
이 문단에서 사용된 감각은 몇 가지?
Q8 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
"My favorite place"를 묘사할 때
가장 잘 쓴 문단은?
