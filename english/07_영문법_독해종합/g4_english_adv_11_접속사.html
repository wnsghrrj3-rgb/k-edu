접속사

and · but · or · so · because + when · before · after

4학년 Track B-1 · 11차시

📖 개념 1 — 등위접속사 (같은 레벨을 연결!)
and 그리고 but 하지만 or 또는 so 그래서
🍕 I like pizza and pasta.
🧠 She is smart but lazy.
☕ Do you want tea or coffee?
😴 I was tired, so I went to bed.
📖 개념 2 — because (이유)
because = ~때문에
이유를 설명할 때 사용해요!
🌧️ I stayed home because it was raining.
😊 She is happy because she got an A.
💡 so = 결과 (그래서) / because = 원인 (~때문에) — 반대 방향!
📖 개념 3 — 시간 접속사
when ~할 때 before ~하기 전에 after ~한 후에
🐕 When I came home, my dog was sleeping.
🧼 Wash your hands before you eat.
🎮 After I finish homework, I play games.
