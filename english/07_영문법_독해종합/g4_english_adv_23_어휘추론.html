📖 개념 — Context Clues (문맥 단서)
모르는 단어 → 주변 문장에서 힌트!
The dog was famished. It hadn't eaten all day and ran straight to its food bowl.
💡 단서: "hadn't eaten all day" + "ran to food bowl" → famished = very hungry!
