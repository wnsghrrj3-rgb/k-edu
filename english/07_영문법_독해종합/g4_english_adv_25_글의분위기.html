독해 전략 9 — 글의 분위기

글의 감정·톤 파악하기

4학년 Track B-2 · 25차시

📖 개념 — Mood & Tone (분위기와 어조)
😊 happy 😢 sad 😱 scary 😲 exciting 😌 peaceful 😂 funny
💡 단서: 사용된 단어(dark, bright, laugh, cry)와 상황을 보고 판단!
Q1 ⭐
The children ran through the meadow, laughing and chasing butterflies. The sun was warm and the sky was clear blue.
이 글의 분위기는?
Q2 ⭐
The old house stood alone on the hill. The windows were broken and the door creaked loudly. A cold wind blew through the empty rooms.
이 글의 분위기는?
Q3 ⭐⭐
The little boy sat by the window, watching the rain. His best friend had moved to another city last week. He held the friendship bracelet and whispered, "I miss you."
이 글의 분위기는?
Q4 ⭐⭐
The lake was perfectly still. Not a single sound could be heard except for birds singing softly in the trees. The morning mist floated gently over the water.
이 글의 분위기는?
Q5 ⭐⭐
The monkey slipped on a banana peel and landed on the elephant's head! The elephant sneezed so hard that the monkey flew into the pond. All the animals burst out laughing.
이 글의 분위기는?
Q6 ⭐⭐⭐
The crowd roared as the runner crossed the finish line first. Fireworks lit up the sky. Her teammates lifted her onto their shoulders. Tears of joy streamed down her face.
이 글의 분위기는?
Q7 ⭐⭐⭐
The forest was dark. Every step made a loud crunch. Something moved behind the trees. Tom's heart beat faster. He heard a low growl. Then... silence.
분위기를 만드는 핵심 단어들은?
Q8 ⭐⭐⭐⭐
Grandma's kitchen was always warm. The smell of fresh bread filled the house every Sunday morning. She hummed old songs while stirring the soup. I sat at the wooden table, watching her wrinkled hands work with love. Those quiet mornings were the happiest moments of my childhood.
이 글의 분위기와 감정을 가장 잘 설명한 것은?
