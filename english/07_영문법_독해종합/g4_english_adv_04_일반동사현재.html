일반동사 현재 완전정복

3인칭 단수 -s · do/does 의문·부정 · 상태동사

4학년 Track B-1 · 04차시

📖 개념 1 — 3인칭 단수 현재형
He / She / It + 동사s
주어가 3인칭 단수일 때, 동사에 -s / -es / -ies를 붙여요!
🎹 She plays piano every day.
📺 He watches TV after dinner.
📚 Tom studies English hard.
⚡ 규칙: 대부분 +s / -ch,-sh,-s,-x,-o → +es / 자음+y → y→ies
📖 개념 2 — do / does 의문·부정

I / You / We / They

Do you like pizza?
I don't like pizza.

He / She / It

Does he like pizza?
He doesn't like pizza.

⚠️ does/doesn't 뒤에는 동사원형! — He doesn't likes → He doesn't like
📖 개념 3 — 상태동사 (State Verbs)
🧠 생각·감정·소유 = 상태동사
상태동사는 진행형(-ing)을 쓸 수 없어요!
know 알다 like 좋아하다 want 원하다 have 가지다 believe 믿다 need 필요하다
✅ I know the answer. (O)
❌ I am knowing the answer. (X)
